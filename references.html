References

Primary bibliography

The primary bibliography includes a selection of articles, papers, and books either written by the author or dedicated to the main theme, the person or the event on which the project concentrates.
In this case, this section provides two websites, showed in alphabetical order, focused on novel entitled I promessi sposi. The 2 websites cited here refer, respectively, to the digital spanish translation of the Ventisettana written by Juan Nicasio Gallego and the digital original Ventisettana written by Alessandro Manzoni, since the objective of the project is to analyze the differences between theese two texts.


  • I Promessi Sposi in Europa e nel mondo - Il tesoro di casa Manzoni
  • Internet Archive

Secondary bibliography

The secondary bibliography includes a selection of articles, papers, and books related to the central theme of the project.
In this case, it is structured it as a broad general section divided into two subsections.
The first one presents a list of websites, arranged in alphabetical order, that addresses documentation about the I promessi sposi novel, offers background on the two authors, Alessandro Manzoni and Juan Nicasio Gallego, whose texts are here compared.
The second one mentions, always in aplhabetical order, some useful, effective and powerful tools i have employed during the developemnt this project and excellent already existing projects in the digital environment. These projects not only deal with both Alessandro Manzoni’s work I promessi sposi, but also offer engaging features such as the abilty to compare the original text in italian and the corrispondent translation in different languages.
